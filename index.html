The Eat’n Park Caring for Kids Campaign is an annual five-week fundraising campaign that benefits children’s hospitals across the communities we serve

Since its inception in 1979, the Caring for Kids Campaign has raised more than $12 million company-wide

The funds raised stay in our region, benefitting more than a dozen children’s hospitals across our company
